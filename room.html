Rooms





At Berlin Homestay, located in the tranquil Kallimali Viewpoint, Idukki, our rooms are designed to provide comfort and relaxation in the heart of nature. Each room is furnished with cozy, simple furnishings, and equipped with a private bathroom to ensure a convenient stay. A table with a view is provided in each room, allowing you to enjoy the breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and valleys as you sip your morning tea or catch up on work. Whether you’re here to explore or simply unwind, our rooms offer a peaceful retreat where you can relax in comfort. Perfect for solo travelers, couples, or families, Berlin Homestay offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all.

Single
